# Game Hat Results

## Overview

The results experience communicates:
- round outcomes
- score updates
- leaderboard state
- next Active Player
- session completion

The experience should feel:
- rewarding
- readable
- socially positive
- fast-moving

---

## Core Principle

Results screens should reinforce social momentum rather than interrupt gameplay flow.

Players should quickly understand:
- who won
- current scores
- what happens next

---

## Round Results

After each round, Players may see:
- winning Player
- Active Player
- revealed prompt
- updated scores
- next Active Player

---

## No-Winner Results

If no Player wins:
- clearly communicate outcome
- preserve social pacing
- continue smoothly into next round

Possible results:
- timeout
- skipped round
- no correct guess

---

## Score Visibility

Scores should remain:
- easy to read
- lightweight
- socially friendly

Avoid:
- overly competitive presentation
- aggressive ranking emphasis
- visually overwhelming leaderboards

---

## Leaderboard Philosophy

The leaderboard should:
- support fun social competition
- avoid toxic pressure
- remain lightweight and readable

The MVP should prioritize:
- simple score totals
- player ordering
- minimal statistics

---

## Session Completion Results

When gameplay ends:
Players may see:
- final scores
- winning Player(s)
- rounds played
- session duration
- replay/rematch options

---

## Replay Flow

After completion:
Possible actions:
- replay same settings
- return to lobby
- modify settings
- exit session

Replay should minimize setup friction.

---

## Visual Tone

Results should feel:
- celebratory
- lightweight
- socially comfortable

Avoid:
- hyper-competitive esports presentation
- excessive animations
- long interruptions between rounds

---

## Shared Device Considerations

Results screens should support:
- quick visibility for all Players
- readable score updates
- easy handoff between rounds

---

## Future Possibilities

Possible future additions:
- round history
- funniest moments
- favorite prompts
- session summaries
- lightweight achievements

---

## Design Principles

- maintain gameplay momentum
- reinforce positive social interaction
- clarity over spectacle
- lightweight score presentation
- replay-friendly flow
